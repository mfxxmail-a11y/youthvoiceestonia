Our Goals

YouthVoice exists with a clear mission — to reshape the relationship between young people and politics in Estonia.

01 🗳️
More young voters

Youth voter turnout in Estonia remains far below its potential. Our goal is to directly raise the number of young people who show up to vote — not just once, but as a habit. Every election is a chance to shape the country you live in.

02 🧠
Educated and conscious votes

Voting without understanding is noise. We want young voters to know what they're choosing — to read critically, verify sources, understand party positions, and make decisions based on facts rather than headlines or social media trends.

03 ✊
Youth can change their future

Politics isn't something that happens to you — it's something you participate in. Housing costs, job markets, climate policy, education funding — these are decided by governments that you elect. Your generation has the numbers to change the outcome.
